Kingsway Fellowship International was born as God moved in the heart of our founder, Dr. D.L. Browning, in 1968.  After having been a Pastor for many years and seeing the heart-felt desire of scores of men and women wanting help and direction for their ministries, God spoke to him to make that possible by providing a Spirit – Filled Ministerial Fellowship, not a denomination, for them.  That was the beginning of KFI.

 KFI began with only a handful of men and women ministers of great vision and we have never allowed our vision to diminish, but rather we have endeavored to fulfill the word given to us in Isaiah 54:2-3, “Enlarge the place of thy tent, and let them stretch for the curtains of thine habitations: spare not, lengthen they cords, and strengthen thy stakes:  For thou shalt break forth on the right and on the left.”  Those founding members were of the “Pentecostal Persuasion” and we continue to strive for the faith once delivered unto the saints until this hour.

 We have grown until today we are a fellowship of over 2500 ministers now affiliated and credentialed with KFI serving on five continents, including many churches, children’s homes, colleges and indigenous pastors and workers.  KFI conducts regularly scheduled conferences, conventions and fellowship meetings throughout the United States and other countries each year.  KFI continues to be committed to fulfill the “Great Commission” in our generation by “preaching the Gospel in all Nations” so that all people may come to faith in Jesus Christ and enjoy the abundant, eternal life found only in Him.
