About Kingsway Fellowship

Kingsway Fellowship was Founded in 1968
Kingsway Fellowship International provides:
Nonprofit status (501 (c) 3) for Churches & Religious Organizations.
A Spiritual Covering for those having their own Tax Exempt Status.
A bond of Fellowship & Unity with people of like-precious Faith.
KFI does not seek to own your ministry properties or assets.
Kingsway has a relationship today with several thousand ministers and missionaries on 5 continents.

In Memory Of KFI Founder - Dr. D. L. Browning

Founder - Dr. D. L. Browning, Th.D.
(1968 - 2000)

KFI was divinely conceived in the heart of God and revealed to its Founder, Dr. D.L. Browning, Th.D., in 1968. Dr. Browning retired from his day to day leadership duties in October 2000. He went to be with his Lord & Savior in January 2006. His motto was "Always keep the main thing, the main thing".
